# Welcome to the World Republic

The World Republic is a band of companies, communities, and citizens working together. The Republic takes an experimental and agile approach to governance, relentlessly iterating towards a system which stands the test of time.
Whether you're a builder curious about how to get a grant, a prospective representative interested in beginning to vote, or looking for ways to contribute to the Republic, you'll find everything you need to get started right here.
